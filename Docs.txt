•PROBLEM STATEMENT 
Maternal healthcare access still to this day remains limited to many women in Bangladesh, especially in the deep rural ends of this nation. Pregnant women often face delayed risk detections, lack of personalized health guidance, misinformation and domestic abuse without any accessible and trustworthy support systems. One of the major issues that is often overlooked is that of post-partum depression that is considered a taboo till this day in Bangladesh. Existing maternal care solutions are often language-restricted, internet-dependent, and not tailored to local healthcare realities. 

•PROPOSED SOLUTION

MaternaAI is an AI-powered healthcare assistant designed to support mothers during the trimesters and post-partum stages of pregnancy. 
- Predictive danger identification using patient history and symptom patterns
-Bangla speech-to-structured medical data conversion
-AI-moderated peer support group for post-partum depression
-Domestic abuse alert and emergency support system
-Personalized nutrition intake recommendations 
-AI-generated birth plan assistance
-Offline mode supported via SMS

•AI ARCHITECTURE/TECHNICAL APPROACH

-LLM powered conversational healthcare assistant
-Bangla speech-to-text conversion pipeline for voice based assistance
-Predictive risk analysis using patient data and medical history without violating personal data
-NLP moderation system to detect misinformation and harmful content
-Emergency alert system in collaboration with law enforcement authorities

•TECH STACK

-FRONTEND- React/ Flutter mobile application
-BACKEND- FASTAPI/ Node.js
-DATABASE- PostreSQL+ cloud based databases for efficient memory storage
-Offline fallback support via collaboration with trustworthy mobile network service providers

•EXPECTED IMPACT AND SCALABILITY 

MaternaAI aims to improve maternal healthcare accessibility for underserved communities of Bangladesh by providing localized and personalized AI-powered support in Bangla. The platform can scale to include:
Rural telemedicine integration
NGO and clinic partnerships after careful selection and verification
Nationwide maternal healthcare and post-partum depression awareness programs
Languages other than Bangla being supported for the ethnic tribes of the rural hilly regions
